# **App Name**: Apex Kirchmair

## Core Features:

- Performance Profile Intake: An interactive form to collect user metrics and fitness history, utilizing an AI tool to suggest a preliminary strategy outline based on their profile.
- Dynamic Consultation Packages: Premium-designed plan selection module for 'Combo' and 'Diet Only' options with real-time price updates and selection highlights.
- Strategic Workflow Card: A high-contrast interactive 'How it Works' card featuring refined animations and step-by-step methodology progression.
- Evolution Gallery: A responsive before-and-after carousel designed for high-impact social proof using mobile-first scrolling optimizations.
- Branded Motion Tickers: Dynamic scrolling marquee strips for reinforcement of premium branding and social authority.
- Professional Bio Suite: Featured presentation section for Robert Kirchmair with image overlays and localized expert highlights.
- Checkout Router: Secure redirection logic to external payment gateways or high-intent communication channels.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Vibrant Kinetic Orange (#FF8A00) used for high-urgency call-to-actions and vital headers to evoke energy and results.
- Background: Near-Black Carbon (#0B0A09) with deep red and royal purple gradients to establish an elite, exclusive aesthetic.
- Accent: Lustrous Gold (#D69A1F) used sparingly for borders and trust markers like the 7-day guarantee icon.
- Headline font: 'Space Grotesk' in bold caps for a modern, computerized, and strong technological presence. Body font: 'Inter' for high legibility and objective professional appeal.
- High-impact editorial layout with large generous padding, centered focus cards, and asymmetric imagery typical of high-end athletic boutiques.
- Minimalist geometric icons with vibrant orange strokes to maintain clarity and high-performance branding.
- Staggered scroll-reveals and fluid transitions between dark sections to mimic a high-end physical fitness environment.
